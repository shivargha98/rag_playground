POLICY CONTRACT: SENTINEL 'PLATINUM' INFINITY
UIN: SHA-HLT-PLT-2025-V5 | CATEGORY: PREMIUM LUXURY
VERSION: 5.0 


SECTION I: THE PROMISE
Sentinel Health Assurance guarantees "As Actuals" coverage for the Insured. The philosophy of this plan is "Medical Necessity over Financial Limits."


SECTION II: UNRESTRICTED BENEFITS
2.1. ROOM CATEGORY
    (a) NO CAPPING. The Insured is eligible for ANY room category provided by the Hospital, including Presidential Suites, Service Apartments, or Deluxe Wards.
    (b) No Proportionate Deduction applicable under any circumstances.


2.2. AIR AMBULANCE COVER
    (a) Domestic: Covered up to Rs. 5,00,000 per hospitalization.
    (b) International: Covered up to Rs. 20,00,000 if treatment is not available locally and requires transfer to a Platinum Network Global Partner.


2.3. WORLDWIDE EMERGENCY COVER
    (a) Coverage extends to Medical Emergencies occurring anywhere in the world (excluding USA/Canada unless "zone extension" premium paid).
    (b) Limit: Up to 100% of Sum Insured for In-patient treatment abroad.
    (c) Deductible: USD 500 per claim for international claims.


SECTION III: HOLISTIC & SPECIALIZED CARE
3.1. MATERNITY & NEWBORN
    (a) Maternity Limit: Rs. 2,00,000 (Normal) / Rs. 3,00,000 (C-Section).
    (b) Waiting Period: Reduced to 12 Months.
    (c) Newborn Cover: Automatic coverage from Day 1 within the Sum Insured. No separate premium for the first year.
    (d) Stem Cell Banking: Covered up to Rs. 15,000.


3.2. AYUSH TREATMENT
In-patient treatment taken under Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy is covered up to 100% of SI, provided treatment is at a Government-recognized AYUSH hospital.


3.3. REHABILITATION & PAIN MANAGEMENT
Covers physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and psychiatric counseling (up to Rs. 50,000) post-hospitalization for trauma/critical illness.


SECTION IV: ADMINISTRATIVE TERMS
4.1. CO-PAYMENT
ZERO CO-PAYMENT. This policy is free from zone-based, age-based, or disease-based co-payment clauses.


4.2. PRE-EXISTING DISEASES
Waiting period for PED is reduced to 12 Months.


SECTION V: PLATINUM PRIORITY NETWORK
Platinum members have access to the "Green Channel" desk at partner hospitals (Direct admission without queue).
