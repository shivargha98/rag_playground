The Transformer architecture has transformed artificial intelligence by introducing the concept of self-attention. 
Transformers process input sequences in parallel, making them more efficient than traditional recurrent models. 
Modern AI systems like GPT and BERT are built on the Transformer foundation, achieving breakthroughs in natural language understanding. 
The scalability and versatility of the Transformer have also inspired innovations in computer vision and multimodal learning.
