SECTION 1: OPERATIVE CLAUSE
The Company undertakes to indemnify the Insured Person against Medically Necessary expenses incurred for In-patient Care, Day Care Treatment, and Domiciliary Hospitalization, subject to the terms and sub-limits herein.


SECTION 2: CORE BENEFITS
2.1. ROOM RENT ELIGIBILITY
    (a) The Policy covers expenses for a "Single Private A/C Room".
    (b) Definition: A standard room with one patient bed, an attached washroom, and a couch for the attendant. It excludes "Suite", "Deluxe", "Super Deluxe", or any category with a separate living area.
    (c) Upgrade Clause: If the Insured opts for a Suite, the Company will reimburse room charges up to the Single Private A/C Room tariff of that specific hospital. However, NO Proportionate Deduction will apply to doctor's fees or ancillary charges.


2.2. RESTORATION OF SUM INSURED
    (a) If the Base SI is exhausted due to claims, the Company will automatically restore 100% of the Base SI once per policy year.
    (b) Trigger: The restoration triggers upon partial or full utilization.
    (c) Restriction: Restored SI cannot be used for the SAME illness/disease for which a claim was already paid in the same year. It applies only to unrelated illnesses.


2.3. NO-CLAIM BONUS (NCB)
    (a) 10% increase in Base SI for every claim-free year, maxing out at 50%.
    (b) If a claim is made, the NCB reduces by the same percentage (10%) in the subsequent renewal.


SECTION 3: TREATMENT-SPECIFIC COVERAGE
3.1. MODERN TREATMENT METHODS
The following procedures are covered up to 50% of the Sum Insured:
    i.  Uterine Artery Embolization
    ii. Balloon Sinuplasty
    iii. Deep Brain Stimulation
    iv. Stereotactic Radio Surgeries
    v.  Intravitreal injections


3.2. ORGAN DONOR EXPENSES
Covered up to the Sum Insured. Includes the cost of harvesting the organ from the donor. Excludes donor's pre/post-hospitalization and cost of the organ itself.


3.3. MATERNITY (OPTIONAL RIDER - NOT STANDARD)
*Unless the "Gold Plus" rider is purchased, Maternity, Childbirth, and newborn baby expenses are EXCLUDED.*


SECTION 4: EXCLUSIONS & WAITING PERIODS
4.1. Standard Waiting Period: 30 days for all illnesses (except Accident).
4.2. Named Ailments (2 Years): Hysterectomy, Knee Replacement, Cataract, Sinusitis, Tonsillitis.
4.3. Pre-Existing Diseases (PED): Covered after 36 continuous months of coverage.
4.4. Excluded Providers: Treatment taken at "Blacklisted Hospitals" (as updated on Company website) is not covered, except in life-threatening accidents.


SECTION 5: NETWORK & CASHLESS
Cashless service is authorized for all hospitals listed in the Gold Network.
    (a) Pre-auth turnaround time: 6 Hours.
    (b) E-Card is mandatory for cashless requests.
